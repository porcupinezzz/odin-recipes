Lasagna recipe

This is an intro to a simple pasta recipe, very popular

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Paste, Tomatoes and Tomato Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Preparation

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Serve
